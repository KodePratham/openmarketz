# Vision

OpenMarketz enables permissionless prediction markets where the creator acts as the oracle. The protocol is designed for niche, local, and community events that curated platforms do not support.

## Core Thesis
- Every meaningful claim can become a market.
- Community trust and visible on-chain history can govern oracle behavior.
- Fast and low-cost execution is mandatory for micro-betting.

## Product Principles
- Permissionless market creation.
- No listing gatekeepers.
- Simple yes/no market structure for V1.
- Transparent, on-chain settlement.
- Reputation-first social trust model.

## V1 Success Outcome
A user can create a market, share an OPEN code, others can bet in MON, creator resolves after close time, winners claim payouts, and protocol fee routes to treasury.
